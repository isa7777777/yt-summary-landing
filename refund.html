Refund Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. 14-Day Refund

If you are not satisfied with our service, you can request a full refund within 14 days of your initial subscription purchase. To request a refund, contact us at shtush76@gmail.com with your User ID or email used for the purchase.

2. Eligibility

Refunds are eligible for:

  • First-time subscriptions only.
  • Within 14 days of the initial purchase date.
  • Subscriptions where you used fewer than 10 summaries.

3. Non-Refundable

Refunds are NOT issued for:

  • Subscription renewals (after the first period).
  • Requests made more than 14 days after purchase.
  • Accounts that have used more than 10 summaries.
  • Accounts terminated for violations of our Terms of Service.

4. Crypto Payments

For payments made via cryptocurrency (NOWPayments), refunds will be issued in the same cryptocurrency, sent to the wallet address you provide. Refund amount may differ from original due to exchange rate fluctuations.

5. Card Payments

For payments made via card (Robokassa), refunds are issued back to the original card within 5-10 business days.

6. How to Request

Email shtush76@gmail.com with:

  • Your User ID (visible in the extension settings) or email used for purchase.
  • Reason for refund (optional but appreciated for our improvement).

We typically respond within 24 hours.
