Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how YouTube Summary ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our browser extension and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Anonymous User ID: When you install the extension, we generate a unique random identifier (UUID) stored in your browser. This ID is not linked to your real identity.
  • Email address: Only collected when you subscribe to a paid plan, used for sending receipts and renewal reminders.
  • Video metadata: When you request a summary, we receive the YouTube video ID and language so our AI can process it.
  • Usage statistics: Number of summaries generated per user, used to enforce subscription limits.
  • Payment information: Processed by our payment providers (Robokassa, NOWPayments). We do not store credit card details.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not track your YouTube viewing history.
  • We do not collect your IP address for tracking purposes.
  • We do not collect personal information beyond what's stated above.
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Generate AI-powered summaries of YouTube videos you request.
  • Enforce subscription plan limits.
  • Send transactional emails (purchase confirmations, renewal reminders).
  • Improve our service through anonymous usage analysis.

4. Third-Party Services

To provide our service, we use the following third-party providers:

  • Supadata: retrieves YouTube video transcripts.
  • DeepSeek: generates AI summaries.
  • Supabase: stores user accounts and usage data.
  • Render: hosts our backend infrastructure.
  • Robokassa, NOWPayments: process payments.
  • Brevo: sends transactional emails.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of marketing emails (transactional emails are required for service operation).
  • Export your data in a portable format.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email to subscribed users.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at shtush76@gmail.com.
